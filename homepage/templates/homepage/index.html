Hello, World!

This has been a labor of love that I began in the Summer of 2016. One day, I hope this site can serve the purpose that Michel de Montaigne's collection of essays intended for him: opening up for the world to see.

A sort of modern, living autobiography.

This site serves as a window into my life. It will be filled with stories of my travels, my take on social and political issues, philosophy, reviews, references, programming projects; truly whatever I want the world to see.

In Montaigne's words: "I want to be seen here in my simple, natural, ordinary fashion, without straining or artifice; for it is myself that I portray."
